Who are we?

Crowdsourced Infrastructure App (CSIA) is a reporting tool built by the people for the people. CSIA allows ease of access to users to report road infrastructure issues. Report issues in your community with ease! Our application enables you to quickly report potholes, broken streetlights, and other public infrastructure problems, helping local authorities take prompt action.
